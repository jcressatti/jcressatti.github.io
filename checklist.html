Weekend Checklist

Checklist

This is by no means an accurate or extensive list of what to bring, merely some ideas and suggestions.

  • Sleeping Bag or Bedding
  • Pillow
  • Cell phone with waterproof case
  • Sun Block
  • Bug Spray
  • Fishing rod and reel
  • Fishing license
  • Multi-tool
  • Pliers
  • Water bottle
  • Advil for pain relief
  • Bandages, medial tape and gauze
  • Hat
  • Long johns
  • Touque
  • Rain gear (jacket and pants)
  • Waterpoof shoes
  • Pants, quick dry are best
  • Socks, bring extra
  • Underwear
  • Shorts
  • Speedo
  • Jacket
  • Running shoes
  • Sunglasses (polarized)
  • Towel
  • Rubber boots/waders
  • Laminated maps
  • Hooks & jig hooks
  • 6-8lb test (mono)
